About Us

In the sun-soaked coastal haven of Southern California, the story of Bountiful Foods begins with a love for both farming and surfing. It all started back in 1980 when the Peterson family, avid surfers and passionate farmers, decided to share their two greatest loves with the world. Nestled in the heart of Carlsbad, they opened their first humble shack, offering up the freshest fruit smoothies crafted from their own farm's bounty.

What began as a small family venture soon blossomed into a beloved local gem, as beachgoers and surfers alike flocked to taste the refreshing goodness of Bountiful Foods' smoothies. With each sip, customers could taste the care and dedication that went into sourcing the finest fruits from local farms.

As word of mouth spread and demand grew, Bountiful Foods expanded its reach, setting up shacks along the stunning coastline of Southern California. From Newport Beach to Long Beach, and all the way down to San Diego, the Peterson family's commitment to quality and their deep connection to both farming and coastal living remained at the heart of their business.

Today, Bountiful Foods stands as a testament to the enduring spirit of family, community, and the simple joys of beach life. With every smoothie served, they continue to honor their roots, celebrating the beauty of Southern California's landscapes and the abundance of its farms. And as the sun sets over the Pacific, one thing remains certain-the legacy of Bountiful Foods will continue to thrive, a symbol of love, flavor, and the coastal lifestyle.
