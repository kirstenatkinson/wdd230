Why join Stockholm's Chamber of Commerce?

Welcome to the Stockholm Chamber of Commerce, your gateway to thriving business opportunities in Sweden's capital! Joining our chamber means unlocking a wealth of benefits tailored to support and elevate your business. Our chamber hosts a myriad of local events designed to showcase and promote small businesses, providing a platform for visibility, growth, and community engagement. Join us today and harness the power of collaboration to propel your business to new heights in Stockholm's dynamic marketplace.

Diversity in Stockholm

About 27% of Stockholmers are immigrants or of non-Swedish background. About 15% of the city is foreign-born, which is the highest of any Nordic city. Many immigrants are concentrated in the city's suburbs, with a wide variety of languages spoken in Greater Stockholm, including: Swedish, Finnish, English, Bosnian, Arabic, Syriac, Kurdish, Persian, Dutch, Turkish, Spanish, Croation and Serbian. The largest foreign-born groups are Finns (18,000), Iraqis (16,400), Iranian (11,600). (worldpopulationreview.com)
