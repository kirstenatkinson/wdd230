Our Goal

Welcome to the Stockholm Chamber of Commerce! Our goal is to serve as the premier resource and advocate for businesses, entrepreneurs, and professionals in Stockholm, Sweden. Through our website, we aim to:

  1. Foster Growth: Provide resources, support, and networking opportunities to foster the growth and success of businesses in Stockholm.
  2. Connect the Community: Facilitate connections and collaboration among members of the business community, promoting innovation and economic development.
  3. Advocate for Change: Advocate for policies and initiatives that create a conducive environment for business growth and prosperity in Stockholm.
  4. Empower Entrepreneurs: Offer guidance, mentorship, and access to resources to empower entrepreneurs to launch and scale their ventures.
  5. Showcase Stockholm: Highlight the diverse and vibrant business landscape of Stockholm, attracting investment, talent, and opportunities to the region.
  6. Provide Information: Serve as a reliable source of information on local regulations, industry trends, and business opportunities in Stockholm.

Together, we aim to build a thriving and inclusive business ecosystem in Stockholm, driving prosperity and innovation for our members and the community at large. Welcome to the Stockholm Chamber of Commerce – where business thrives!

Get on the Map

By joining the Stockholm Chamber of Commerce, businesses gain access to powerful advertising opportunities and impactful events that can significantly elevate their visibility and presence in the market. Our chamber hosts a variety of events, including networking mixers, industry conferences, and trade shows, where members have the opportunity to connect with potential partners, clients, and collaborators. These events provide a platform for businesses to showcase their expertise, forge valuable connections, and establish themselves as key players in the Stockholm business community. By leveraging our advertising platforms and participating in our events, members can effectively "get on the map" and position themselves for sustained growth and success in the vibrant market of Stockholm.
